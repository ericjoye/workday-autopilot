# Workday Autopilot — Landing Page Copy

## Headline

**Stop filling out the same job application 50 times.**

## Subhead

Workday Autopilot is a Chrome extension that auto-fills and auto-submits Workday job applications with one click. Your profile, your rules, done in seconds — not minutes.

## Benefit Bullets

- **One-click apply, actually done** — Autopilot detects every field on a Workday application, fills it from your saved profile using React-compatible injection (no more ignored fields), and submits. A 5-minute form becomes a 3-second click. You review before it sends — no surprises.

- **Works on every Workday career page** — Whether it's a Fortune 500 company or a university HR portal, if it runs Workday, Autopilot works. Smart field detection handles names, emails, phone numbers, addresses, LinkedIn URLs, dropdowns, and work experience — even on dynamically-loaded forms.

- **Free to start, powerful when you need more** — 5 free submissions per month, no credit card. Pro ($14.99/mo) unlocks unlimited submissions and priority support. Team ($29/mo) adds shared profiles for recruiters and career coaches managing multiple candidates.
